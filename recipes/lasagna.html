Lasagna

Description

A lentil lasagna with tofu ricotta and marinara sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 2 25 ounce jars marinara sauce
  • 1 cup raw cashews
  • 16 ounce firm tofu, pat dry with paper towels
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2-3 cups baby spinach
  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • Dairy free mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lentils. Add 1 cup dried lentils to 3 cups water and bring to a boil. Simmer for 20 minutes. Drain in a fine strainer.
  2. Add strained lentils and marinara sauce to a large bowl and combine, then set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  4. Make tofu ricotta. Add cashews to food processor and blend until fine. Add tofu in chunks, along with nutritional yeast, lemon juice, salt, basil, oregano and garlic powder. Pulse until smooth and well combined.
  5. Assemble lasagna. Add 1 cup of lentil sauce to the bottom of a large casserole dish. Spread around evenly and then add a layer of the uncooked noodles. Spread the ricotta on the noodles and top with the spinach. Repeat this process and place the last noodles over the spinach. Pour the rest of the sauce evenly over the top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 40 minutes. Remove from oven and add mozzerella, and bake another 20 minutes.
  7. Let cool for 15 minutes before serving.
