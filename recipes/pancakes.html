Banana Pancakes

Description

A simple, three ingredient pancake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 tbsp ground flax mixed with 2 tbsp water (rested for 5 minutes)
  • 2 tbsp wholemeal self rising flour

Steps

  1. Mash banana in a medium bowl and whisk in flax mixture and flour until smooth. Set aside for 5 minutes.
  2. Lightly oil frying pan at medium heat. Spoon 2 tbsp of the batter into the pan, speading with spoon until a 1/2 inch thick. Repeat for other pancakes.
  3. Cook for 1-2 minutes on both sides until golden and cooked through. Serve.
