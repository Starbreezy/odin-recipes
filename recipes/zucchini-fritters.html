Zucchini Fritters

Description

A quick and easy recipe made from fresh zucchini and chickpea flour. Great as a side dish and an alternative breakfast idea.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • 3 cups grated zucchini
  • 3/4 to 1 cup chickpea flour
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard (optional)
  • 3-4 tbsp chives or sliced green onions (green parts only)
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp dill seed
  • black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Stir the lemon juice with the tahini.
  2. Mix the other ingredients in a large bowl and pour over the lemon-tahini mixture.
  3. Combine until you have a uniform mixture, then let it sit for 15 to 20 minutes.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat
  5. Scoop about 1/4 cup of the mixture onto the skillet and flatten into a disc shape.
  6. Cook fritter for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through. If they are browning too quick, reduce heat to allow them to cook more thoroughly.
  7. Serve immediately.
